Safety Management System (SMS) Development & Safety Consulting

Ensuring safety and regulatory compliance is at the heart of Learnmarine’s consulting services. We offer full-scale SMS development, implementation, and training to meet international safety standards, including the ISM Code.

  1. Customized Safety Management Systems

    We develop tailored SMS manuals for individual vessels or entire fleets, ensuring compliance with international maritime safety standards and operational efficiency.

  2. SMS Integration & Training

    Our team provides hands-on support to integrate SMS seamlessly into daily operations while delivering crew training on risk management, emergency response, and compliance.

  3. Audit & Regulatory Compliance Support

    We assist maritime companies in preparing for and successfully passing audits such as Class, Flag, OVID, and RightShip, ensuring full compliance with global safety regulations.

  4. Continuous Improvement & Regulatory Updates

    We conduct regular internal audits and annual SMS reviews, ensuring that safety protocols remain up to date with the latest industry regulations and best practices.
